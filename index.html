Colourful, vibrant, and full of ancient royal splendour, Rajasthan is the largest state of India . Rich culture, heritage, traditional warmth and finest hospitality makes it very popular among travellers from all around the world. There are several monuments, palaces, forts, havelis, etc showing the glorious past and history. Wildlife sanctuaries and national parks are also the prime attractions of Rajasthan tourism. Rajasthan is one of the most popular states for travellers to visit in India. The state is known for its beautiful palaces, hotels, forts, food, wildlife and heritage. Various cities of Rajasthan house buildings of different colour. Jaipur is called Pink city, Jodhpur is called Blue City whereas Udaipur is called White city of Rajasthan. The state has prominent national parks like Ranthambore National Park (known for Tigers), Jawai Camp (Known for Leopards) and Sariska National Park (known for its Deers, Boars etc). Jaisalmer is one of the most beautiful cities of Rajasthan located in Thar desert. You can experience the desert life, havelis and colourful markets in this region.
